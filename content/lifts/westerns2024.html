Meet recap of the 2024 Canadian Powerlifting Union Western Canadian Championships, held in Moose Jaw, Saskatchewan. I cut weight for this meet, weighing in at 65.34 kg.

Squat:
  1. Attempt 1: 182.5 kg
  2. Attempt 2: 192.5 kg
  3. Attempt 3: 200 kg (pictured)
Bench Press:
  1. Attempt 1: 110 kg
  2. Attempt 2: 117.5 kg
  3. Attempt 3: 122.5 kg (pictured)
Deadlift:
  1. Attempt 1: 235 kg (failed)
  2. Attempt 2: 235 kg
  3. Attempt 3: 265 kg (failed, pictured)

Bittersweet meet performance. I hit a nice milestone of a 3x bodyweight squat, but had some more left in the tank and should have pushed it more. Also almost hit a 4x bodyweight deadlift, but I couldn't hold on to it at the top. Working on making sure my grip is more consistent.

Aside from my own performance, I had an amazing time. Watching high level lifters in person is really inspiring and tells you that the sky is the limit when it comes to progress.
