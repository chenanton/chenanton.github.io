An Iterative Bayesian Approach to Premise Generation with Entailment Trees

Paper and repo.

We propose a Bayesian approach to premise generation by constructing a probability model on entailment trees, iterative sampling an entailment tree bottom-up from initial premises i.e. leaf nodes. We expand on previous work in the area by exploring premise retrieval premise generation through a Bayesian lens, defining priors and likelihoods on retrieved and generated premises. We implement the model with approximate variational methods in Pyro, a PPL for Python.
